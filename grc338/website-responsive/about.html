Our Mission

Sunshine Coffee Co. is dedicated to bringing ethical craft-roasted coffee to your cup. We are committed to sourcing only the finest organic, fair-traded coffee beans from small-lot indigenous farmers who share our vision. We believe that specialty coffee is for everyone, and should be accessible, approachable and fun. It is our privilege to find beautiful coffees and roast them and serve them in our hometown of San Luis Obispo. If you are in town, stop by and say hi!

While we are the last hands that touch these coffees, we cannot claim to be solely responsible for their deliciousness. Coffee is the product of many human beings hard work: from farm to mill to the exporter to roaster; this chain works when we are all invested in each other. We are committed to working with importing and exporting partners that are invested in the long-term success of the coffee producers.

Sunshine Coffee Co. Established 2021.
